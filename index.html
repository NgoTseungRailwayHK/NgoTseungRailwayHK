About us

Ngo Tseung Railway (2016-), is a railway company operating service in both Minecraft Java Edition and Bedrock Edition. Now providing services at Project Nazca Server (Mosaic Island), Project Akiba Server and Windwood. We’re trying our best to bring the best service to all the passengers and creating a better railway network.

Now, Ngo Tseung Railway develops in different divisions and servers. For example, we developed our first railway – Dormon Railway in Dormon division, developing Siu Mai (小迷), Tin Yan (天欣) divisions in Minecraft Bedrock Edition. After several years, our railway system become more mature. We started to install platform screen doors and platform gates in our stations’ platforms, adding dynamic speed systems and setting up depart routines, to bring the best service ever to our passengers.


Project Nazca Server (Mosaic Island)

It is the first division that Ngo Tseung Railway developed in Minecraft: Java Edition. It was developed based on Light Rail system to serve residents and visitors in Mosaic Island.
